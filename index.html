    About

    Hello and thanks for visiting my homepage. My name is Min-kyeong (Min) Cha, and I am LSE-Newcleo Fellow in Energy Economics and Policy at the London School of Economics and Political Science. I completed my PhD at the Carter School of Public Policy, Georgia Institute of Technology.

    With my interdisciplinary background, my research focuses on energy and environmental policy, employing econometric methods, statistical models, and machine learning techniques. Specifically, I am specifically interested in topics such as renewable energy policy, electricity markets, adoption of clean energy technologies, innovation in energy technology, and energy consumption behaviour.

    I was BBISS Graduate Fellow and EPICenter Student Affiliate at Georgia Institute of Technology. I was also a Fulbright student for the 2020-2022 academic year.

    I love to connect with people for exciting projects! Please do not hesitate to contact me at: m.cha1 [at] lse.ac.uk
